ABOUT

My name is Xavier REBUFFONI, I am currently in my first year of BUT MMI (Métier du Multimédia et de l'Internet) in Montbéliard. In this course we are trained to design websites and ensure their promotion. This website is therefore one of the works to be carried out as part of our training.

In the begenning, the subject of this project was "Note for later", inspired by Orelsan’s music entitled "Note pour trop tard". The goal is to talk to our future self by stating fears, hopes, etc... I decided to interpret this topic by making a site on the theme of global warming and nature. I find this topic particularly interesting in the actual context. This topic is also interesting to share with future generations because they are the ones who will suffer the consequences of global warming.
