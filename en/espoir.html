THE NATURE : OUR LAST HOPE ?

But not everything is lost, there is always hope. This hope is represented by nature. If we live in harmony with it rather than destroy it, we could change the situation. The challenge of this century is to realize all the things that nature can bring to us. And this future is not just a fantasist story. It can really exist if we act.

There is a lot of fears about the future of Earth and humanity. But despite all these fears, there is still hope and we should focus our mind on this point. My dream is to see a planet wher humans have finally a peacefull relation with nature. This peace must be between us but mostly with nature and biodiversity. During many centuries, we exploit Earth without any respect or remorse. Today, we have to change this. The planet has many other things than just ressources for our gadgets.

" The planet has many other things than just ressources for our gadgets. "

If we focus ourself on all these things around us we would see how beautiful the planet is. All the life and all the diversity of landscapes she has.

So yes, I want to bring up my children in a world where people become aware of that. A world where people will have become aware of the fact that we can’t waste all these wealth without any consequences. A planet where people live in harmony with nature, fauna, flora and overall the Earth. A world where humanity will take back there place among all the species that inhabit this Earth. If we live in harmony with nature we can stop the global warming but we can also learn about here to change society.
